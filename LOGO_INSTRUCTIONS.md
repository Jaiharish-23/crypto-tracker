# JHGNO Logo Setup Instructions

## Your Custom Logo
Your beautiful bull and bear logo with the JHGNO coin needs to be saved manually.

### Steps to Add Your Logo:

1. **Save the logo image you provided** as:
   ```
   public/jhgno-logo-main.png
   ```

2. **The logo features:**
   - Bull and bear symbols (market indicators)
   - Golden JHGNO coin in center
   - Purple and blue accents
   - "CRYPTO TRACKER" text

3. **Where the logo appears:**
   - ✅ Login page (large, animated)
   - ✅ Sidebar (medium size)
   - ✅ Mobile header
   - ✅ Favicon (optional: create 32x32px version)

### Logo Specifications:
- **Recommended size**: 512x512px (square)
- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **File size**: < 500KB for optimal loading
- **Background**: Transparent preferred

### Quick Setup:
1. Right-click your logo image
2. Save as `jhgno-logo-main.png`
3. Place in the `public` folder
4. Refresh your browser - logo will appear!

### Fallback:
If the logo file is missing, the app will show a purple/blue gradient icon as fallback.
